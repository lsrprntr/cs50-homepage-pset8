Easy Banana Bread Recipe!
Ingredients
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
Method
  1. Mix all in bowl.
  2. Bake for 1 hour at 165°C
